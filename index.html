About Dustyn

A Leader Ready to Serve

20 Years of Professional Excellence

Dustyn Dorn brings two decades of experience as an IT professional to his campaign for Pennsylvania House of Representatives District 60. His expertise spans troubleshooting, IT service management, vendor relationship management, and Market Data.

As a Democrat running for the PA House, Dustyn is committed to working across party lines to pass legislation that truly benefits all Pennsylvanians. His goal is clear: increase trust in government through transparency and accountability while improving the quality of life for every citizen.

A dedicated family man and community member, Dustyn understands the challenges facing Pennsylvania families and is ready to bring his problem-solving skills to Harrisburg.
